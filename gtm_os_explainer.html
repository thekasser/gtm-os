Operating System

40 GTM services + 2 Brain Agents across 10 layers — from market sizing through reasoning. Hybrid B2B SaaS. Click any layer to explore.

Three tiers. 40 GTM Services in L1–L8 (deterministic, single-writer-per-table, audit-grade). 2 Brain Agents in L9 (LLM-native, operator-invoked, read Tier 1 services but never write canonical data; gated by 30-question eval harness — both prototyped, eval-passing). 12 Specialist Tools in Tier 3 across 3 waves — stateless callable functions that brains and services invoke for narrow cognition tasks (TOOL-003, TOOL-004, TOOL-008, TOOL-010 prototyped; remaining 8 specced). The distinction is deliberate; brains propose, humans co-define and approve, services execute. See changelog for current state.
