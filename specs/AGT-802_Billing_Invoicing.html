AGT-802 — Billing & Invoicing

Layer 8: Revenue Operations · Invoice generation · Consumption billing · SOW milestone billing · Credit memos & refunds · Finance-owned
L8 · Agent 02 Specced · v25 Schema adds: InvoiceLog, InvoiceLineItems, CreditMemoLog
Purpose

AGT-802 owns the full invoice lifecycle — generation, delivery, credit memos, and refunds. It reads OrderLog and OrderLineItems (AGT-801) as the source of truth for what to bill, reads UsageMeteringLog for consumption amounts, and reads TechnicalMilestoneLog (AGT-602) for SOW milestone completion signals. It never decides what was sold — it executes billing against what AGT-801 approved.

SOW milestone billing

Professional services billing is milestone-based, not ratable. AGT-802 holds SOW line item invoices until a milestone completion signal is received.

Signal sourceHow it works
AGT-602 TechnicalMilestoneLogWhen IE marks a milestone complete and customer signs off (customer_sign_off = TRUE), AGT-602 writes the milestone record. AGT-802 polls TechnicalMilestoneLog for new completions and triggers the corresponding invoice per ImplementationSOW payment schedule.
Manual Finance entryFinance can directly mark a milestone as billable in ImplementationSOW when AGT-602 is not active or for non-technical milestones (e.g. project kickoff, go-live acceptance). AGT-802 treats manual and AGT-602 signals identically.
Customer sign-off is required on the TechnicalMilestoneLog record before AGT-802 invoices. AGT-802 never auto-invoices a milestone on IE completion alone — customer acceptance is the billing gate.
