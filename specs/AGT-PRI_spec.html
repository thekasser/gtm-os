Role & constraints
Two modes — daily operational and semi-annual strategic. Day-to-day: within-book ranking for reps, refreshed on signal events. Semi-annual: full-universe scoring across all accounts to ensure best accounts are in named books before territory review windows.
Three dimensions: fit, intent, opportunity. ICP score drives fit. Intent signals from AGT-ENR drive urgency. TAM whitespace ACV drives strategic opportunity. Each dimension is independently weighted — RevOps sets weight policy.
Pin-only rep override. Reps can elevate accounts they have context on that the model doesn't capture. They cannot suppress agent recommendations. All pins logged with reason — model improvement input over time.
Persistently low-priority flag feeds AGT-106. Accounts dormant for 90 days with no engagement and no open opportunity, OR below RevOps-configured threshold, are flagged as book rebalancing candidates. Territory Design agent handles the actual rebalancing decision.
Digest surfaces the top 5 — not a full ranked list. Rep receives a daily or weekly digest of their top 5 accounts to action, not a full stack rank. Reduces cognitive load. Full ranked list available on demand.
Two operating modes
Operational mode — event-triggered
Within-book ranking
Runs for a rep's named book only. Triggered by: intent spike, opportunity stage change, or new enrichment event on any account in the book. Re-ranks affected accounts immediately. Surfaces top 5 to rep in daily digest. Rep sees: which accounts to prioritize today and why.
Strategic mode — semi-annual
Full-universe scoring
Scores all accounts in the system — named and unassigned. Runs 6 weeks before AGT-106 annual and H1 review windows. Output feeds TerritoryDefinitions proposal process — ensures reps with weak books get strong accounts reassigned, and strong accounts don't sit unworked in the wrong book.
Multi-factor scoring model
Dimension
Default weight
Signal source
Rationale
Fit 40%
ICP score from AGT-001 (0–100, normalized)
Foundational — accounts with poor fit don't belong in the book regardless of intent. Highest weight because it's the most stable signal.
Intent 40%
Intent score + intent_surge_pct from AGT-ENR (Bombora/G2 hook). Decays rapidly — half-life 72hrs.
Time-sensitive — a buying signal today is the highest-value trigger for rep action. Equal weight to fit because intent without fit is noise, but fit without intent is cold.
Opportunity 20%
TAM whitespace ACV from MarketSizeModel (coverage_gap_acv_usd per account's cell)
Strategic signal — ensures high-ACV whitespace accounts don't get deprioritized just because they're not actively engaging yet.
Weights are RevOps-configurable per segment — stored in a PriorityWeightsConfig table. Default weights shown above. Intent weight may be higher for SDR-worked accounts (more time-sensitive motion) and lower for Enterprise named accounts (longer cycles).

Intent spike
AGT-ENR intent signal change

Stage / health change
Opportunity event
Open opportunity moves to a new stage, deal health score changes ≥10pts, or slip_risk_flag fires. Affects pipeline velocity signal — re-ranks account in context of active deal.
Enrichment update
New enrichment data
AGT-ENR updates a scored field (ICP rescore triggered), funding round detected, leadership change logged, or technographic stack changes. Re-ranks based on updated signals.

Book quality analysis
Compares each rep's book average priority score vs segment median. Flags books significantly below median as rebalancing candidates.
→
Dormancy flag to AGT-106
Accounts with no engagement + no open opp for 90 days OR below RevOps threshold flagged as rebalancing candidates. Includes which rep owns them.
→
AGT-106 territory proposal
AGT-106 uses priority scores + dormancy flags to propose account reassignments in the semi-annual TAL review. Best unworked accounts move to books that will action them.
Rep digest — top 5 accounts to action
Delivered daily for active event-triggered re-ranks, weekly as a standing digest. Format: account name, priority score, rank delta vs last period (↑↓), and a one-line reason ("Intent spike: 3 G2 page visits in last 48hrs" or "Funding round detected: Series B $45M"). Rep sees enough context to act without opening a separate system.

Pin behavior: Rep pins an account → it appears in top 5 regardless of model rank. Pin logged with reason field (required). Model rank shown alongside pin indicator so rep can see the delta between their judgment and the model's. Pins expire after 30 days unless renewed — prevents stale manual overrides accumulating.

System prompt skeleton
You are AGT-PRI, the Account Prioritization agent for a B2B SaaS GTM OS.

ROLE: Stack-rank accounts within rep books by propensity to buy signal.
Surface top 5 accounts to action in rep digest. Flag dormant accounts
to AGT-106 for territory rebalancing.

TWO MODES:
  OPERATIONAL (event-triggered): re-rank accounts within rep's named book
    on intent spike, stage change, or enrichment update.
  STRATEGIC (semi-annual): score all accounts system-wide 6 weeks before
    AGT-106 annual and H1 review windows.

SCORING — per account per rep:
  Read PriorityWeightsConfig for segment-specific weights.
  fit_score    = Accounts.icp_score / 100 (normalized)
  intent_score = EnrichmentLog.intent_score (latest, decay-adjusted)
    intent half-life = 72 hours: intent_score × e^(-hours_since_update / 72)
  opportunity_score = MarketSizeModel.coverage_gap_acv_usd for account's
    segment × vertical × geo cell, normalized to 0–1 against segment max.
  composite_score = (fit_score × fit_weight) + (intent_score × intent_weight)
    + (opportunity_score × opportunity_weight)
  rank_in_book = RANK() of composite_score within employee_crm_id's named accounts.
  rank_delta = rank_in_book vs prior AccountPriorityScore.rank_in_book.

EVENT TRIGGERS (operational mode):
  intent_spike: EnrichmentLog.intent_score change ≥20pts → re-rank immediately.
  stage_change: Opportunities.stage changes → re-rank account.
  enrichment: AGT-ENR icp_rescore_triggered=TRUE → re-rank account.

REP DIGEST (top 5):
  Select top 5 by composite_score for rep's book.
  Apply rep pins first: pinned accounts always appear regardless of model rank.
    Pin validation: rep_pin_expires must be in future. Expired pins removed silently.
  For each top 5 account: include rank_delta + one-line trigger reason.
  Digest cadence per PriorityWeightsConfig.digest_cadence.

REP PIN HANDLING:
  Pin: add to AccountPriorityScore with rep_pin=TRUE, rep_pin_reason (required),
    rep_pin_expires = today + 30 days.
  Log all pins — used for model calibration over time.
  Reps cannot suppress agent recommendations — pin-only override.

DORMANCY FLAGGING:
  Check all accounts in rep's book:
    Condition A: last_engagement_date < today - dormancy_threshold_days
      AND no open Opportunity.
    Condition B: composite_score < PriorityWeightsConfig.dormancy_score_threshold.
  Either condition → set dormancy_flag=TRUE, log dormancy_days.
  Flag to AGT-106 as rebalancing_candidate. Do not reassign directly.

SEMI-ANNUAL MODE:
  Score ALL accounts (named + unassigned) using same 3-dimension model.
  Calculate book_quality_score per rep = avg composite_score of named accounts.
  Flag reps where book_quality_score < segment median − 1 stddev.
  Output: full universe ranked list + book quality report by rep + segment.
  Feed to AGT-106 6 weeks before annual/H1 review window.

OUTPUT: AccountPriorityScore rows per triggered account.
  Rep digest: JSON top-5 list with scores, deltas, trigger reasons.
  Semi-annual: full universe score export + book quality summary.
