Purpose

AGT-803 monitors payment status across all invoices, orchestrates payment retries on failure, escalates delinquencies through the appropriate notification chain, and writes payment_health_status to CustomerHealthLog as a read-only feed for AGT-501. It is the source of the payment health modifier that caps or floors AGT-501 health scores. Every payment event — success, failure, retry, escalation — is logged in PaymentEventLog.

Payment health states
StateDefinitionAGT-501 modifier
CurrentAll invoices paid within terms. No outstanding failures.No effect on health score.
OverdueInvoice past due date. Payment failed on retry 1 or 2. Still in retry window.Health score capped at 77 — cannot reach Low risk tier regardless of other dimensions.
FailedAll 3 retries exhausted. Invoice unpaid. Awaiting Finance intervention.Health score capped at 62 — cannot reach Medium tier.
SuspendedFinance has suspended account access pending payment resolution.Health score floored at Critical (0–44) regardless of all other dimensions.
States are per-account, not per-invoice. An account with one overdue invoice is in Overdue state even if other invoices are current. State resolves to Current only when all outstanding invoices are paid.
Retry logic
AttemptTimingEscalation
Initial payment attemptOn invoice due dateNone on success. Failure triggers Retry 1.
Retry 110–20 minutes after initial failureFinance notified. Payment status → Overdue. No AM notification yet.
Retry 2Next business dayFinance notified (updated status). No AM notification yet.
Retry 3Following business day (2 business days after initial failure)Finance + AM notified simultaneously on failure. Payment status → Failed.
Post-retry (Failed)No further automatic retriesFinance owns manual collection process. SLM notified. AM has visibility.
SuspendedFinance-initiated — no automatic triggerFinance + AM + SLM notified. AGT-501 modifier → floor at Critical.
Retry 1 uses the same payment method as the initial attempt. Retries 2 and 3 may use an alternate payment method if one exists on file — Finance can configure fallback payment methods per account.

Finance is always the first notified. AM involvement begins at Failed status — the relationship owner needs to know before the customer realizes access may be affected. AGT-501 modifier fires immediately on status change, meaning the account's health score adjusts before the AM's first notification arrives.
AGT-501 integration

AGT-803 writes payment_health_status to CustomerHealthLog on every status change. This is a non-blocking read-only feed — if AGT-803 is not live, AGT-501 skips the payment modifier and scores on the 7 behavioral dimensions alone. When live, AGT-501 applies the modifier last in its scoring sequence (after all dimension scores are computed) so the cap/floor is visible as a distinct adjustment in the log.

Production controls & audit (v30)

AGT-803 sits at the intersection of finance and customer-success workflows. Its outputs gate AGT-501's health score (cap/floor model) and trigger AM/SLM notifications on delinquency. Wrong payment state misroutes account interventions; missed retries cost cash. Controls below are mandatory.

Edge cases & failure modes
ScenarioBehavior
Account has multiple open invoices: one Current, one OverdueAccount state = Overdue (worst applicable wins). AGT-501 modifier caps health at 77. State recovers to Current only when both invoices are paid.
Customer pays Failed invoice but new invoice goes Overdue same dayState remains Overdue (newly Overdue invoice continues to gate). AM may have been notified on prior Failed; new state change does not re-notify (same state class).
External billing system processes payment but fails to send confirmationDaily reconciliation (per AGT-802 spec) detects discrepancy. AGT-803 holds state on affected invoices until reconciliation resolves; AM not notified prematurely.
Customer changes payment method during Overdue stateRetry resumes with new payment method on next scheduled retry. State transitions normally on success or failure. PaymentEventLog records method change.
Account has alternate payment methods on fileRetry 1 uses primary; Retry 2 and 3 may use alternate per Finance configuration. Payment method used logged per attempt.
Delinquency leads to Suspended state mid-billing-cycleAGT-501 modifier floors health at Critical. AGT-502 churn risk may already be elevated. AGT-504 customer comm sequenced (with human approval). AGT-503 expansion plays auto-suspended for the account.
Previously Suspended account is reactivatedFinance manually clears Suspended state with rationale. AGT-501 modifier removed in next health refresh. Account returns to behavioral health scoring; AGT-503/AGT-502 resume normal operation.
External billing system reports payment success then later reverses (fraud, chargeback)AGT-803 receives reversal event, transitions account back to appropriate state (typically Failed). PaymentEventLog records the reversal as a distinct event. AGT-802 may issue compensating credit memo.
AGT-803 offline during payment failure eventExternal billing system retains the failure event; AGT-803 ingests on recovery. State updates correctly applied; AM notification timing may slip but accuracy preserved.
