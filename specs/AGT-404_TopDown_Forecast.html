Update — FP&APlan versioning (v21 schema)
FP&APlan now carries plan_version, reforecast_as_of_date, and is_board_plan fields. AGT-404's feasibility comparison must reference the current operating plan — not the original 0+12 board plan — as pipeline trajectory evolves through the year.

Rule: AGT-404 reads FP&APlan WHERE is_board_plan = FALSE ORDER BY reforecast_as_of_date DESC LIMIT 1 for the operating plan comparison. Board plan is included as a secondary reference line in the output (labeled separately) so Finance and CRO can see both: "where we are vs what we told the board" vs "where we are vs our current internal target."

Output adds: plan_version label on every comparison line so the reader knows which plan version the % refers to. Avoids confusion when operating plan has been reforecast mid-year.
Purpose

AGT-404 produces the top-down forecast — a pipeline cohort conversion model that derives a revenue projection from historical conversion rates applied to current pipeline by cohort. It runs independently of AGT-402 (bottoms-up) and the two are reconciled in AGT-704 WBR/MBR synthesis. AGT-404 expresses its projection as a % of FP&APlan target, providing a board-ready on-track/off-track narrative alongside the forecast number.

Cohort conversion model

Pipeline is segmented into cohorts. Historical conversion rates are applied per cohort to derive a close probability independent of rep-entered stage or deal health scores.

Cohort dimensionPurpose
Segment (SMB / MM / Enterprise)Conversion rates differ materially by segment — Enterprise deals close at lower rates but higher ACV
Stage entered + time in stageDeals that have lingered in a stage beyond historical median convert at lower rates
