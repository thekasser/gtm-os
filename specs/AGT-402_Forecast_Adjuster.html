L4 ripple audit updates (v23)
Update 1 — FP&APlan versioning (v21 schema)
FP&APlan now carries plan_version (0+12, 1+11, 2+10, etc.), reforecast_as_of_date, and is_board_plan fields. AGT-402's pct_of_target field must join on the current operating plan version — not the board plan and not the original 0+12 plan.

Rule: AGT-402 reads FP&APlan WHERE is_board_plan = FALSE ORDER BY reforecast_as_of_date DESC LIMIT 1 to get the current operating plan. Board plan is available as a secondary reference only — not the primary denominator for pct_of_target.
Update 2 — Expansion ACV included in bottoms-up forecast (locked decision)
AGT-402's bottoms-up forecast now includes three revenue components: new logo pipeline, renewal pipeline, and expansion ACV from AGT-503 ExpansionLog. Expansion ACV is read from ExpansionLog.acv_estimate where play_type = 'new_play' and suppressed_reason IS NULL. Expansion ACV is probability-weighted by churn risk tier: Low = 80%, Medium = 50%, High/Critical = 0% (suppressed by AGT-503 already, but defensive guard).

Output format adds an expansion revenue line to the forecast breakdown alongside new logo and renewal. pct_of_target denominator uses the full FP&APlan revenue target (which includes planned expansion revenue).
Purpose

AGT-402 produces the bottoms-up AI forecast — an agent-adjusted view of pipeline that incorporates deal health scores, rep commit patterns, and historical conversion rates. It supplements rep commits with a probability-weighted pipeline view and expresses both against FP&APlan targets. Rep commit is sacred — AGT-402 models commit separately, never overrides it. Material deals are binary scenarios, not probabilistic.

New logo pipelineOpportunities — new business dealsProbability-weighted by AGT-401 deal health score + historical conversion rate by stage, segment, vertical
Renewal pipelineOpportunities — renewal deals + ChurnRiskLogAGT-401 deal health + AGT-502 churn risk tier used to adjust renewal probability. Critical/High churn risk deals discounted.
Expansion ACVExpansionLog (AGT-503) — active plays onlyacv_estimate weighted by churn risk tier: Low = 80%, Medium = 50%, High/Critical = 0%
Rep commitRep-entered commit field on OpportunitiesPresented as-is. Never adjusted by agent. Displayed alongside AI forecast for comparison.
Material deals (>25% of segment quota) are modeled as binary scenarios — closed/not closed — not assigned a probability. These are flagged separately in the forecast output and excluded from the probability-weighted total to avoid distorting the AI number.
pct_of_target output

Every forecast run outputs pct_of_target by segment — AI forecast total ÷ current operating FP&APlan revenue target. Three reference points always included:

ReferenceSourceNotes
Current operating planFP&APlan — most recent non-board versionPrimary denominator. Rolls from 0+12 → 1+11 → 2+10 as actuals close.
Board planFP&APlan WHERE is_board_plan = TRUESecondary reference. External benchmark for CRO and Finance — not used as denominator.
Prior period AI forecastPrior forecast run outputWeek-over-week and month-over-month trend. Sourced from previous AGT-402 output record.
