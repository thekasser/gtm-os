Voice of Customer Agent
Synthesizes customer feedback signals from six sources into structured themes and actionable insights. Monthly deep synthesis with a lighter weekly digest. Five consumer outputs: Product, RevOps, Marketing, Finance/RevOps, and CRO. Closes the feedback loop from customer experience back into L0 policy tables (PricingConfig review) and L2 models (ICP refinement, MarketAssumptions).
New agent New tables: VoCSynthesisLog, VoCSignalLog Feeds back: AGT-201 ICP model, PricingConfig (L0), MarketAssumptions (AGT-205)

Synthesis cadence
Monthly deep synthesis
Full synthesis across all 6 signal sources. Produces structured theme clusters: feature requests ranked by frequency + customer profile, pricing feedback patterns, ICP refinement signals (which firmographic profiles generate best outcomes), messaging gaps from objection patterns, and the CRO org-wide summary. Writes to VoCSynthesisLog. Distributed to all 5 consumer outputs.
Weekly digest
Lighter pass covering new ConvIntelligence signals, support ticket spikes, and any NPS/CSAT data received in the prior week. Not a full synthesis — flags anomalies and emerging themes to relevant consumers only. Writes a digest record to VoCSynthesisLog with synthesis_type = 'weekly_digest'. CRO summary is monthly only.
Consumer outputs — 5 recipients
Product team
Feature request themes ranked by frequency and customer profile weight. Adoption gap analysis — which capabilities are available but underutilized, with hypothesis on why. Monthly deep synthesis + weekly anomaly flags.
Feeds: product roadmap prioritization (external to GTM OS)
RevOps
ICP refinement signals: which customer firmographic profiles (segment × vertical × size band) generate the highest product adoption, lowest churn risk, and fastest TTV. Flags when ICP model assumptions may be drifting from observed outcomes.
Feeds: AGT-201 ICP Scorer weight recalibration, AGT-205 MarketAssumptions update
Marketing
Messaging gaps: objection patterns from ConvIntelligence that suggest messaging isn't landing. Competitor mention themes. Language customers use to describe value (vs language marketing uses) — useful for copy and positioning.
Feeds: messaging refresh cycles (external to GTM OS)
Finance / RevOps
Pricing feedback signals: patterns in pricing objections, value perception vs price point, which SKUs generate most friction. Structured as a PricingConfig review input — not a recommendation, a signal set for Finance to evaluate.
Feeds: PricingConfig review cycle (L0). Finance decides; VoC surfaces the signal.
CRO — org-wide summary
Monthly only. Top 5 customer themes across the full book. NPS/CSAT trend. Leading retention risk signals. Expansion momentum indicators. Written as an executive narrative — not raw data. Designed to inform CRO's customer perspective in board and leadership conversations.
Feeds: AGT-702 GTM Health Monitor (customer sentiment as a health signal)
